    Please enter your ship placements. You have an aircraft carrier (5 spaces long), a battleship (4 spaces long), and a submarine (3 spaces long). These will be placed upon a 10x10 grid with values A-J and 0-9. Enter "A", "B", or "S" followed by a range of grid spaces for your aircraft, battleship, and submarine respectivley. Place a semicolon between ship placements.

    Valid entry formats include:
  • A:A1-A5;B:B6-E6; S:H3-J3
  • A(A1-A5); B(B6-E6); S(H3-J3)
  • B(B6-E6);S(H3-J3);A(A1-A5)
